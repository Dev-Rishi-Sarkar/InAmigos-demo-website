About the NGO

Who they are

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla. It is based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh and is known for its public-impact work across India.

Section 8 80G & 12A CSR-1 ISO 9001:2015 NGO Darpan
