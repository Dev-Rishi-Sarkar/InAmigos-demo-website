About the foundation

A short, accurate overview of the NGO’s identity, registrations, and mission.

Overview

Built for social good

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact through education, healthcare awareness, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare and community development.

It was founded on 23 September 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla and operates from Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

Credentials

Recognitions & registrations

Section 8 80G 12A CSR-1 ISO 9001:2015 NGO Darpan

The foundation’s public presence shows a strong focus on community-driven solutions and sustainable service through volunteers and partner networks.

Mission

Make support practical

To bridge gaps in education, health awareness and economic opportunity for underprivileged communities using grassroots action and collaboration.

Vision

Build an inclusive future

To help create a society where every person can learn, grow, contribute and live with dignity.
