Ongoing projects

A clear summary of the NGO’s main initiatives and the social purpose behind each one.

Project Bachpanshala

Supports underprivileged children through school learning support, basic digital literacy and life-skill development.

Project Udaan

Empowers women through skill training, confidence-building and pathways to financial independence. The foundation’s blog highlights that Udaan has already transformed the lives of 900+ women.

Project Jeev

Focuses on animal welfare and care, including support for stray animals through volunteer action and feeding efforts.

Project Prakriti

Promotes environmental conservation, sustainability awareness and planting activities that strengthen a greener future.

Project Seva

Supports community needs through food distribution, clothing support and outreach for people facing hardship.

Project Vikas

Builds employability through internships, learning opportunities and skill development for youth and students.

Why it matters

Social impact in one line

These projects create a chain of change: children learn better, women earn better, communities receive support, animals are cared for and the environment is protected.
